Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about working with Central Strategies—certifications, contracting, our services, and how to get started.

Can’t find what you need? Contact us and we’ll respond promptly.

What is Central Strategies’ socioeconomic certification?
Central Strategies is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB). We maintain current registration and certifications needed for federal and agency set-aside opportunities, including a valid UEI and CAGE code for government contracting.
Which industries and agencies do you support?
We work with U.S. government departments and agencies, integrators, and enterprise organizations. Our focus areas include cybersecurity, cloud, AI/ML, data analytics, enterprise IT, automation, systems engineering, and financial compliance aligned with federal standards.
Do you staff cleared personnel?
Yes. We maintain a bench of cleared professionals and can support programs requiring Secret, Top Secret, and TS/SCI access subject to customer facility and program requirements. Tell us your clearance and location needs during intake.
How do engagements typically start?
Most projects begin with a discovery call to align on mission, constraints, and success criteria. From there we may propose a statement of work, task order support, or staff augmentation depending on your acquisition vehicle and timeline.
Can you support both public-sector and commercial clients?
Yes. We deliver superior services and results to both the public sector and the private sector, applying the same emphasis on talent development, security, and measurable outcomes.
Where is your team located?
We support customers nationally with concentration in the Washington DC–Baltimore corridor and remote-friendly delivery models. Specific onsite requirements are coordinated per contract.
How quickly can you respond to a new inquiry?
For general inquiries we aim to respond within one to two business days. For urgent procurement or security-sensitive topics, note that in your message so we can prioritize appropriately.
Do you partner with other primes or small businesses?
We regularly team with primes, SDVOSBs, and other socioeconomic partners on GWACs, IDIQs, and agency-specific vehicles. Reach out to discuss subcontracting or joint pursuit opportunities.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to walk through your requirements and how we can help.
